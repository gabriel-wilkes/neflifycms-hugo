About

Azul Technology was started in 2007 by Gabriel Wilkes. Azul Technology was primarily focused on IT for small business in the early days. However, those same small businesses also needed websites and Gabriel has been making websites since the early days of the public internet in 1995.

Over time, due to changing interest and technologies available, web development and e-commerce became the primary focus. If you would like to learn more please contact me or visit the social links at the bottom of the page. Thanks for stopping by!
